# Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) — Identity Governance & IGA Pillar

## 1. Overview & Purpose

Identity Governance & IGA ensures **comprehensive, compliant, and secure** identity management across the enterprise. This SOP protects against identity-related risks, access violations, and ensures adherence to identity governance best practices, access management, and regulatory requirements.

**Key Threats Addressed:**
- Identity theft and impersonation attacks
- Privileged access abuse and insider threats
- Identity lifecycle management failures
- Access certification and attestation gaps
- Identity compliance violations and audit failures
- Identity provisioning and deprovisioning failures
- Identity analytics and risk assessment gaps
- Identity governance policy violations
- Identity data quality and integrity issues
- Identity integration and federation failures

---

## 2. Scope

* All **identity management systems** across the enterprise
* All **identity governance** and access management
* All **identity compliance** and regulatory frameworks
* All **identity analytics** and risk assessment
* All **identity security testing and validation**

## 3. Roles & Responsibilities

### **Strategic Roles**

* **Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)** → defines identity governance strategy, risk appetite, and governance
* **Principal Identity Governance Architect** → designs comprehensive identity governance architecture and controls
* **Head of Identity Governance / IGA Manager** → owns operational execution and compliance
* **Chief Technology Officer (CTO)** → ensures identity governance integration in development processes
* **Head of Risk & Compliance** → ensures adherence to identity governance standards and regulations

### **Execution Roles**

* **Identity Governance Engineer** → implements identity governance controls, access management, and monitoring
* **Identity Lifecycle Manager** → manages identity lifecycle and provisioning
* **Access Certification Specialist** → manages access certification and attestation
* **Identity Analytics Specialist** → manages identity analytics and risk assessment
* **Identity Governance Security Tester** → conducts identity governance security testing and vulnerability assessments
* **Identity Compliance Analyst** → ensures identity governance compliance and regulatory adherence
* **Identity Data Quality Specialist** → manages identity data quality and integrity
* **Incident Responder (Identity Governance)** → handles identity governance breaches and incidents

### **Appendix B: Identity Governance Security Incident Severity Levels**

**Critical (P1):**
- Successful identity governance system compromise with data loss
- Identity governance system failure with access impact
- Identity theft with business impact
- Identity governance system outage with business impact

**High (P2):**
- Multiple identity governance vulnerabilities with security impact
- Identity governance control failures with access violations
- Identity governance compliance violations with regulatory impact
- Identity governance system performance issues with security impact

**Medium (P3):**
- Single identity governance vulnerability with limited impact
- Identity governance security issues without access impact
- Identity governance control issues without security impact
- Identity governance compliance gaps without regulatory impact

**Low (P4):**
- Identity governance security monitoring alerts and notifications
- Identity governance configuration issues without security impact
- Identity governance security training gaps and awareness
- Identity governance security documentation updates
