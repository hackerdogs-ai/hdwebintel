# Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) — Threat Intelligence Pillar

## 1. Overview & Purpose

Threat Intelligence ensures **proactive, informed, and strategic** cybersecurity posture across the enterprise. This SOP protects against emerging threats, advanced persistent threats (APTs), and ensures adherence to threat intelligence best practices, threat hunting, and regulatory requirements.

**Key Threats Addressed:**
- Advanced persistent threats (APTs) and nation-state attacks
- Emerging threat actors and attack techniques
- Threat intelligence gaps and visibility issues
- Threat hunting and detection failures
- Threat intelligence sharing and collaboration gaps
- Threat intelligence analysis and assessment failures
- Threat intelligence integration and automation failures
- Threat intelligence compliance and regulatory violations
- Threat intelligence quality and accuracy issues
- Threat intelligence dissemination and actionability failures

---

## 2. Scope

* All **threat intelligence** across the enterprise
* All **threat hunting** and detection systems
* All **threat intelligence compliance** and regulatory frameworks
* All **threat intelligence analytics** and assessment
* All **threat intelligence testing and validation**

---

## 3. Roles & Responsibilities

### **Strategic Roles**

* **Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)** → defines threat intelligence strategy, risk appetite, and governance
* **Principal Threat Intelligence Architect** → designs comprehensive threat intelligence architecture and controls
* **Head of Threat Intelligence / Threat Intelligence Manager** → owns operational execution and compliance
* **Chief Technology Officer (CTO)** → ensures threat intelligence integration in development processes
* **Head of Risk & Compliance** → ensures adherence to threat intelligence standards and regulations

### **Execution Roles**

* **Threat Intelligence Engineer** → implements threat intelligence controls, threat hunting, and monitoring
* **Threat Hunter** → manages threat hunting and detection activities
* **Threat Intelligence Analyst** → manages threat intelligence analysis and assessment
* **Threat Intelligence Integration Specialist** → manages threat intelligence integration and automation
* **Threat Intelligence Tester** → conducts threat intelligence testing and vulnerability assessments
* **Threat Intelligence Compliance Analyst** → ensures threat intelligence compliance and regulatory adherence
* **Threat Intelligence Sharing Specialist** → manages threat intelligence sharing and collaboration
* **Incident Responder (Threat Intelligence)** → handles threat intelligence breaches and incidents

**Critical (P1):**
- Successful threat intelligence system compromise with data loss
- Threat intelligence system failure with security impact
- APT attack with business impact
- Threat intelligence system outage with business impact

**High (P2):**
- Multiple threat intelligence vulnerabilities with security impact
- Threat intelligence control failures with data exposure
- Threat intelligence compliance violations with regulatory impact
- Threat intelligence system performance issues with security impact

**Medium (P3):**
- Single threat intelligence vulnerability with limited impact
- Threat intelligence security issues without data exposure
- Threat intelligence control issues without security impact
- Threat intelligence compliance gaps without regulatory impact

**Low (P4):**
- Threat intelligence monitoring alerts and notifications
- Threat intelligence configuration issues without security impact
- Threat intelligence training gaps and awareness
- Threat intelligence documentation updates
