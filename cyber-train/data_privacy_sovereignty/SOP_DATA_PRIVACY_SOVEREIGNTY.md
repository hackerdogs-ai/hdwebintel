# Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) — Data Privacy & Sovereignty Pillar

## 1. Overview & Purpose

Data Privacy & Sovereignty ensures **secure, compliant, and sovereign** data handling across the enterprise. This SOP protects against data privacy violations, sovereignty breaches, regulatory non-compliance, and ensures adherence to privacy frameworks, data sovereignty requirements, and regulatory standards.

**Key Threats Addressed:**
- Data privacy violations and unauthorized data access
- Cross-border data transfer violations and sovereignty breaches
- Regulatory compliance failures (GDPR, CCPA, LGPD, PIPEDA)
- Data subject rights violations and consent management failures
- Data localization and sovereignty requirement violations
- Privacy by design implementation gaps and weaknesses
- Data anonymization and pseudonymization failures
- Privacy impact assessment (PIA) and data protection impact assessment (DPIA) gaps
- Third-party data sharing and processing violations
- Data retention and deletion policy violations

---

## 2. Scope

* All **personal data processing** across the enterprise
* All **cross-border data transfers** and international data flows
* All **data sovereignty** and localization requirements
* All **privacy compliance** and regulatory frameworks
* All **data privacy security testing and validation**

## 3. Roles & Responsibilities

### **Strategic Roles**

* **Chief Privacy Officer (CPO)** → defines privacy strategy, risk appetite, and governance
* **Principal Data Privacy Architect** → designs privacy-by-design architecture and controls
* **Head of Data Privacy / Privacy Manager** → owns operational execution and compliance
* **Chief Technology Officer (CTO)** → ensures privacy integration in development processes
* **Head of Risk & Compliance** → ensures adherence to privacy regulations and standards

### **Execution Roles**

* **Data Privacy Engineer** → implements privacy controls, data protection, and compliance
* **Privacy Compliance Analyst** → manages privacy compliance and regulatory adherence
* **Data Protection Officer (DPO)** → oversees data protection and privacy compliance
* **Privacy Impact Assessment Specialist** → conducts PIAs and DPIAs
* **Data Subject Rights Manager** → manages data subject rights and consent
* **Cross-Border Data Transfer Specialist** → manages international data transfers
* **Privacy Security Tester** → conducts privacy security testing and assessments
* **Incident Responder (Privacy)** → handles privacy breaches and incidents

**Critical (P1):**
- Successful privacy breach with sensitive data exposure
- Cross-border data transfer violation with regulatory impact
- Data subject rights violation with legal consequences
- Privacy system outage with business impact

**High (P2):**
- Multiple privacy violations with regulatory impact
- Privacy control failures with data exposure
- Privacy compliance violations with regulatory impact
- Privacy governance violations with compliance impact

**Medium (P3):**
- Single privacy violation with limited impact
- Privacy security issues without data exposure
- Privacy control issues without regulatory impact
- Privacy compliance gaps without regulatory impact

**Low (P4):**
- Privacy monitoring alerts and notifications
- Privacy configuration issues without regulatory impact
- Privacy training gaps and awareness
- Privacy documentation updates
