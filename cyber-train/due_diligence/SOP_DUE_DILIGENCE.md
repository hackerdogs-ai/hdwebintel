# Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) — Due Diligence Pillar

## 1. Overview & Purpose

Due Diligence ensures **thorough, comprehensive, and risk-aware** security assessments across the enterprise. This SOP protects against security blind spots, compliance gaps, and ensures adherence to due diligence best practices, risk assessment, and regulatory requirements.

**Key Threats Addressed:**
- Security assessment gaps and blind spots
- Compliance violations and regulatory failures
- Third-party security risks and vendor vulnerabilities
- Merger and acquisition security risks
- Investment security risks and portfolio vulnerabilities
- Supply chain security risks and dependency failures
- Regulatory compliance failures and audit issues
- Security governance gaps and control weaknesses
- Risk assessment failures and security misconfigurations
- Due diligence process failures and assessment gaps

---

## 2. Scope

* All **security assessments** across the enterprise
* All **third-party security evaluations** and vendor assessments
* All **merger and acquisition** security due diligence
* All **investment security** and portfolio assessments
* All **due diligence testing and validation**

---

## 3. Roles & Responsibilities

### **Strategic Roles**

* **Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)** → defines due diligence strategy, risk appetite, and governance
* **Principal Due Diligence Architect** → designs comprehensive assessment architecture and controls
* **Head of Due Diligence / Risk Assessment Manager** → owns operational execution and compliance
* **Chief Technology Officer (CTO)** → ensures due diligence integration in development processes
* **Head of Risk & Compliance** → ensures adherence to due diligence standards and regulations

### **Execution Roles**

* **Due Diligence Engineer** → implements assessment controls, evaluation frameworks, and monitoring
* **Third-Party Security Assessor** → conducts vendor security assessments and evaluations
* **M&A Security Specialist** → manages merger and acquisition security due diligence
* **Investment Security Analyst** → conducts investment security assessments and portfolio evaluations
* **Due Diligence Security Tester** → conducts due diligence security testing and vulnerability assessments
* **Compliance Assessment Specialist** → manages regulatory compliance assessments and evaluations
* **Risk Assessment Analyst** → conducts risk assessments and security evaluations
* **Incident Responder (Due Diligence)** → handles due diligence failures and assessment incidents

### **Appendix B: Due Diligence Security Incident Severity Levels**

**Critical (P1):**
- Successful due diligence system compromise with data loss
- Due diligence system failure with assessment impact
- Third-party security violation with business impact
- Due diligence system outage with business impact

**High (P2):**
- Multiple due diligence vulnerabilities with security impact
- Due diligence control failures with assessment violations
- Third-party compliance violations with regulatory impact
- Due diligence system performance issues with assessment impact

**Medium (P3):**
- Single due diligence vulnerability with limited impact
- Due diligence security issues without assessment impact
- Due diligence control issues without security impact
- Due diligence compliance gaps without regulatory impact

**Low (P4):**
- Due diligence security monitoring alerts and notifications
- Due diligence configuration issues without security impact
- Due diligence security training gaps and awareness
- Due diligence security documentation updates
