## 1. Roles & Ownership

**Strategic Roles (Oversight & Governance)**

* **CISO / CRO:** Accountable for overall program assurance and risk reduction.
* **Board Cybersecurity Committee:** Ensures security due diligence at governance level.
* **Head of GRC:** Designs compliance and assurance frameworks.
* **Enterprise Risk Officer:** Integrates cyber due diligence into enterprise risk management.

**Execution Roles (Operational & Audit)**

* **Due Diligence Manager:** Coordinates pillar-level reviews, collects metrics.
* **GRC Analysts:** Track compliance, KRIs, and control testing across all pillars.
* **Internal Audit Team:** Performs program-wide assurance reviews.
* **External Auditors:** Validate effectiveness of due diligence controls.
* **Business Unit Security Champions:** Provide local control evidence and feedback.

---

## 2. Role Tasks & Cadence

**Daily Tasks**

* Track control metrics from all pillars (Due Diligence Manager).
* Monitor for exceptions and non-compliance tickets (GRC Analyst).
* Escalate systemic gaps (SOC + GRC).

**Weekly Tasks**

* Review KPI dashboards for all pillars.
* Validate remediation progress for outstanding audit findings.
* Communicate major issues to CISO.

**Monthly Tasks**

* Conduct cross-pillar control validation workshops.
* Report pillar maturity scores to leadership.
* Perform vendor due diligence checks.

**Quarterly Tasks**

* Conduct enterprise risk and compliance committee reviews.
* Validate regulatory reporting alignment (PCI DSS, SOX, HIPAA, GDPR, NIS2, DORA).
* Audit a sample of pillar implementations for consistency.

**Yearly Tasks**

* Commission external audits for program-wide due diligence.
* Deliver cybersecurity annual report to the board.
* Perform a maturity model assessment (e.g., NIST CSF, CMMC, ISO 27001).
* Refresh due diligence framework based on new threats/regulations.
