# Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) — Encryption Pillar

## 1. Overview & Purpose

Encryption ensures **secure, compliant, and protected** data across the enterprise. This SOP protects against data breaches, unauthorized access, and ensures adherence to encryption best practices, key management, and regulatory requirements.

**Key Threats Addressed:**
- Data breaches and unauthorized data access
- Encryption key compromise and key management failures
- Weak encryption algorithms and implementation vulnerabilities
- Encryption key rotation and lifecycle management failures
- Hardware security module (HSM) vulnerabilities and failures
- Encryption performance and scalability issues
- Regulatory compliance failures and audit issues
- Encryption key escrow and recovery failures
- Quantum computing threats and post-quantum cryptography
- Encryption policy violations and governance gaps

---

## 2. Scope

* All **data encryption** across the enterprise
* All **encryption key management** and lifecycle systems
* All **hardware security modules (HSM)** and key storage
* All **encryption compliance** and regulatory frameworks
* All **encryption security testing and validation**

---

## 3. Roles & Responsibilities

### **Strategic Roles**

* **Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)** → defines encryption strategy, risk appetite, and governance
* **Principal Encryption Architect** → designs secure encryption architecture and controls
* **Head of Encryption / Key Management Manager** → owns operational execution and compliance
* **Chief Technology Officer (CTO)** → ensures encryption integration in development processes
* **Head of Risk & Compliance** → ensures adherence to encryption standards and regulations

### **Execution Roles**

* **Encryption Engineer** → implements encryption controls, key management, and monitoring
* **Key Management Specialist** → manages encryption keys and lifecycle
* **HSM Administrator** → manages hardware security modules and key storage
* **Encryption Security Tester** → conducts encryption security testing and vulnerability assessments
* **Cryptographic Specialist** → manages cryptographic algorithms and implementations
* **Encryption Compliance Analyst** → ensures encryption compliance and regulatory adherence
* **Post-Quantum Cryptography Specialist** → manages quantum-resistant encryption
* **Incident Responder (Encryption)** → handles encryption failures and key compromise incidents
