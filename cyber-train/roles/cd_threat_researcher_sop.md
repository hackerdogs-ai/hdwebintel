# Threat Researcher Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

## 1. Purpose and Scope

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) defines the operational framework for Threat Researchers specializing in threat intelligence research, campaign analysis, and threat actor profiling. This SOP ensures consistent, high-quality threat research practices across all security operations.

## 2. Roles and Responsibilities

### 2.1 Threat Researcher Responsibilities
- Conduct threat intelligence research and analysis
- Perform campaign tracking and attribution
- Profile threat actors and analyze behavior
- Gather and collect intelligence information
- Assess threat landscape and provide reporting
- Ensure compliance with intelligence and security standards
- Mentor teams on threat research techniques

### 2.2 Key Stakeholders
- **Threat Intelligence Teams**: Primary collaborators for intelligence analysis
- **Incident Response Teams**: Partners for threat response and containment
- **Security Operations Teams**: Collaborators for threat detection and response
- **Intelligence Teams**: Partners for intelligence sharing and collaboration
- **Management**: Stakeholders for threat research program reporting
