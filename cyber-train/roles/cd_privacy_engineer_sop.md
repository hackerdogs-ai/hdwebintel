# Privacy Engineer Standard Operating Procedure

**Role:** Principal Privacy Engineer  
**Mission:** Design, implement, and maintain privacy protection measures to ensure data protection, privacy by design, and regulatory compliance.  
**Scope:** Enterprise-wide privacy engineering, data protection, privacy impact assessment, and compliance management.

---

## 1. Role Charter & Authority

### Principal Privacy Engineer (PPE) Responsibilities

* **End-to-End Privacy Engineering:** Design → Build → Test → Deploy → Monitor privacy protection capabilities
* **Data Protection:** Design, build, and maintain data protection and privacy systems
* **Privacy Impact Assessment:** Conduct comprehensive privacy impact assessments and risk analysis
* **Deliverable Production:** Create all privacy engineering documentation, reports, and compliance recommendations
* **Privacy DRI:** Act as privacy decision-maker for data protection and privacy compliance

### Authority & Guardrails

* **Privacy Authority:** Can design and implement privacy protection measures
* **Data Protection Authority:** Can implement data protection controls and measures
* **Compliance Authority:** Can ensure compliance with privacy regulations and standards
* **Escalation Authority:** Can escalate privacy risks and compliance issues to privacy leadership

## 6. Exception Management & Escalation

### Privacy Exceptions
- **Criteria:** Only for business-critical operations with compensating controls
- **Documentation:** Exception Record (risk assessment, compensating controls, approval, expiry ≤90 days)
- **Approval:** Privacy leadership approval required
- **Renewal:** Barred without reassessment and re-approval

### Escalation Triggers (Immediate Response Required)
- Critical privacy risks requiring immediate response
- Data breach or data exposure indicators
- Privacy compliance violations or audit failures
- Regulatory privacy violations or enforcement actions
- Executive or management privacy concerns

### Escalation Chain
**PPE → Privacy IC/CPO → Executive Leadership**

**Deliverable:** Privacy Incident Response Report (incident summary, response actions, containment, lessons learned) within 24 hours

## 10. Incident Response Integration

### Privacy Incident Types
- **Critical:** Data breaches, privacy violations, compliance failures
- **High:** Privacy policy violations, consent management failures
- **Medium:** Privacy procedure violations, minor compliance issues
- **Low:** Minor privacy incidents, false positives

### Response Procedures
1. **Detection:** Privacy monitoring, privacy risk detection, incident reporting
2. **Assessment:** PPE assesses incident severity and impact
3. **Activation:** Activate incident response team and procedures
4. **Investigation:** Conduct privacy engineering analysis and root cause analysis
5. **Containment:** Implement containment and remediation measures
6. **Recovery:** Restore operations with enhanced monitoring
7. **Lessons Learned:** Post-incident review and process improvement
