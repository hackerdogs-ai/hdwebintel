# Threat Intelligence Engineer Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

## 1. Purpose and Scope

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) defines the operational framework for Threat Intelligence Engineers specializing in threat intelligence collection, analysis, and dissemination. This SOP ensures consistent, high-quality threat intelligence practices across all security operations.

## 2. Roles and Responsibilities

### 2.1 Threat Intelligence Engineer Responsibilities
- Collect and analyze threat intelligence from various sources
- Profile threat actors and conduct attribution analysis
- Assess threat landscape and provide intelligence reporting
- Facilitate intelligence sharing and collaboration
- Conduct threat modeling and risk assessment
- Ensure compliance with intelligence and security standards
- Mentor teams on intelligence operations

### 2.2 Key Stakeholders
- **Security Operations Center (SOC)**: Primary consumers of threat intelligence
- **Incident Response Teams**: Partners for threat response and containment
- **Threat Hunting Teams**: Collaborators for threat hunting activities
- **Executive Management**: Stakeholders for strategic intelligence reporting
- **External Partners**: Collaborators for intelligence sharing

## 3. Threat Intelligence Process

### 3.1 Planning Phase
- **Intelligence Requirements**: Define intelligence requirements
- **Source Identification**: Identify intelligence sources
- **Collection Planning**: Plan intelligence collection activities
- **Resource Planning**: Allocate resources for intelligence activities
- **Timeline Planning**: Plan intelligence activities timeline

### 3.2 Collection Phase
- **Source Access**: Access intelligence sources
- **Data Collection**: Collect intelligence data
- **Information Validation**: Validate collected information
- **Data Processing**: Process collected data
- **Storage**: Store intelligence data

### 3.3 Analysis Phase
- **Data Analysis**: Analyze collected intelligence
- **Pattern Recognition**: Recognize threat patterns
- **Trend Analysis**: Analyze threat trends
- **Attribution**: Attribute threats to actors
- **Impact Assessment**: Assess threat impact

### 3.4 Production Phase
- **Report Generation**: Generate intelligence reports
- **Briefing Preparation**: Prepare intelligence briefings
- **Recommendation Development**: Develop intelligence recommendations
- **Quality Assurance**: Ensure intelligence quality
- **Documentation**: Document intelligence products

### 3.5 Dissemination Phase
- **Stakeholder Communication**: Communicate with stakeholders
- **Information Sharing**: Share intelligence information
- **Collaboration**: Collaborate with partners
- **Feedback Collection**: Collect feedback on intelligence
- **Documentation**: Document dissemination activities

### 3.6 Evaluation Phase
- **Intelligence Assessment**: Assess intelligence effectiveness
- **Process Review**: Review intelligence processes
- **Lessons Learned**: Document lessons learned
- **Process Improvement**: Improve intelligence processes
- **Training**: Provide training based on findings
