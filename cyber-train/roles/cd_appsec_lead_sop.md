# Application Security Lead Standard Operating Procedure

**Role:** Principal Application Security Lead  
**Mission:** Lead and manage comprehensive application security programs, drive security strategy, and ensure organizational security maturity.  
**Scope:** All application security programs, security teams, strategic planning, and executive security leadership.

---

## 1. Role Charter & Authority

### Principal Application Security Lead (PAL) Responsibilities

* **Strategic Leadership:** Develop and execute comprehensive application security strategies and roadmaps
* **Program Management:** Lead and manage enterprise application security programs
* **Team Leadership:** Build, develop, and lead high-performing security teams
* **Stakeholder Management:** Collaborate with engineering, product, and executive teams
* **Risk Management:** Strategic risk assessment and mitigation planning
* **Compliance Leadership:** Ensure adherence to security standards and regulatory requirements

### Authority & Guardrails

* **Strategic Decision Making:** Authority to make strategic security decisions and program changes
* **Resource Allocation:** Authority to allocate security budgets and team resources
* **Policy Development:** Authority to develop and implement security policies and procedures
* **Vendor Management:** Authority to evaluate and manage security vendors and partners
* **Executive Reporting:** Authority to report directly to C-level executives and board members

## 6. Exception Management & Escalation

### Strategic Exceptions
- **Criteria:** Only for business-critical strategic decisions
- **Documentation:** Exception Record (risk assessment, business justification, mitigation plan)
- **Approval:** Executive leadership approval required
- **Review:** Quarterly review and renewal required

### Escalation Triggers (Immediate Response Required)
- Major security incidents affecting business operations
- Regulatory compliance violations
- Significant budget overruns or resource issues
- Critical team performance or retention issues
- Major vendor or technology failures

### Escalation Chain
**PAL → CISO → CEO → Board of Directors**

**Deliverable:** Executive Incident Report (impact assessment, response actions, lessons learned) within 24 hours
